Rules of The Game

Let's suppose we have three points (we'll call them anchor points) in the real plane along with an inkdropper. Figure 1 below shows the anchor points in black and the inkdropper in the middle. Our game progresses simply: an anchor point is chosen at random, and the inkdropper travels halfway towards the targeted anchor point.
